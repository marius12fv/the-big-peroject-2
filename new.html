Why is important to practice Pingpong?

Aside from being a fun way to spend an afternoon with your family and friends, table tennis also offers surprisingly great health benefits. Like most sports, table tennis offers great mind-body stimulation, aerobic exercise, and social interaction. Unlike many sports, however, the overall risk for injury with table tennis is quite low.

⇨A list why you should to practice pingpong⇦

  • Improving hand-eye coordination
  • Improving reflexes
  • It’s easy on the joints
  • It keeps your brain sharp

Why is important to playing Baseball?

America’s favorite pastime BASEBALL is still the leading youth sport among teens across the United States. Baseball is a great way to teach them the value of teamwork. Not to mention, it is a great way to stay healthy and physically fit when played on a regular basis. The cardio exercise strengthens your heart muscle and improves lung capacity, which you get from running around the field. Batters running the bases, outfielders running to catch a fly ball and catchers chasing a foul ball all get short bursts of cardiovascular exercise.

⇨A list why you should to play⇦

  • Builds Strength and Endurance
  • Improves Mental Focus
  • Teaches Teamwork
  • Enables Players to Build Confidence in Themselves
